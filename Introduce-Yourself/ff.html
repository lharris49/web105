Fantasy Football Fanatic

About Lenny World Traveler Fantasy Football

Since I was a young kid, I always loved the NFL. I grew up watching my San Francisco 49ers in the 80’s as they won five Super Bowls. Unfortunately, when I was stationed overseas during the mid-90’s, I started to lose track of what was going on. About that same time, a new craze was taking hold – fantasy football. So, I joined a league and found a new passion!

With the growth of the Internet, fantasy football flourished. It is now a multi-million-dollar industry. Around the year 2000 or so, I joined an online league with a group of my military buddies, the Buncha Non-Ball Playing Mo Fo’s league. Although all but one of the team “owners” is now out of the military, our league is still going strong two decades later. It’s a great way for all of us to stay in touch and razz each other a little bit.

Below are the standings from our most recent season (my team is the Lensters). After a 13-week regular season, the top eight teams face off in a three-week playoff bracket. This season’s champion was the Gridiron Monsters.
